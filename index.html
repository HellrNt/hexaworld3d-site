About HEXA WORLD 3D

HEXA WORLD 3D is a cozy yet competitive 3D hexagonal puzzle game. Every level is procedurally generated to adapt to your progress. Earn XP, unlock maps and skins, use powerful boosters, and compete in global leaderboards. Choose your mode: Infinity (relax), Competitive (5-min score challenge), or Level Mode (XP & loot grind).

Core Features

Procedural 3D Puzzle Gameplay

Each level is uniquely generated based on your level and progress, ensuring endless variety.

Three Game Modes

Infinity Mode for chill play, Competitive Mode with leaderboards, and Level Mode to earn XP and loot.

XP Progression & Unlocks

Gain XP and unlock new hex skins, maps, and tactical boosters as you level up.

Global Leaderboards

Compete for high scores in the monthly Competitive Mode and track your overall progress.

Strategic Boosters

Use the Hammer, Swap, Unlocker, or Field Cleanser to solve tricky puzzles and set records.
